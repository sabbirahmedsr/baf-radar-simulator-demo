The Airport Diorama

A physical tabletop runway where every landing and take-off of an aircraft in the simulator is mirrored by a real-world model, perfectly synchronized in real-time.

The Vision

  • Landing Scenario: As a virtual aircraft approaches the 6 DME mark, a physical model instantly appears on the diorama to mirror the final descent. After touchdown, it clears the runway and disappears, ready for the next arrival.
  • Departure Scenario: When an aircraft lines up, its physical counterpart moves into position, accelerates for take-off, and vanishes from the diorama as it becomes airborne in the simulation.

Implementation Plan

  • Connect: Establish a connection between this web software and an Arduino microcontroller via WebSockets or WebUSB.
  • Build: Construct a physical tabletop runway and small robotic platforms to carry miniature aircraft models.
  • Sync: Translate simulator data (position, heading) into movement commands for the physical robots.
